Contacts

This group is dedicated to showing the human side of why a pathway to Bermuda Status/ PRC needs to be implemented. Many people who have ties to Bermuda do not have any possibility of becoming a citizen of the country that they consider home. We all know someone in this situation and now we have an opportunity to change it.

Please share your stories

Please share or resend your stories to the three independent senators Carol Bassett, Joan Dillas-Wright and James Jardine. We need to let them know how you feel about pathways to status so they can vote in favor. If you haven't shared your story with the other MP's please send it to them as well. The independent senators email addresses are:

Carol Bassett: cbassett@parliament.bm

Joan Dillas-Wright: JDillas-Wright@parliament.bm

James Jardine: jjardine@parliament.bm


Please share this information but don't forget to email them.
